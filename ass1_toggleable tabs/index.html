SDP projects are focused on software development and involve building real-world applications using industry-standard tools and techniques.

EDI projects involve the use of electronic data interchange (EDI) to improve business-to-business communication and automate business processes.

DT projects involve the application of design thinking methodologies to solve complex problems and create innovative solutions.

Course projects are assigned as part of VIT's curriculum and are designed to help students apply the knowledge and skills they have learned in their courses.
